Anthem is focused on sovereignty, science, technology, ecology, philosophy, and new forms of living.

It publishes essays, studies, concepts, and visual work on how people might build, inhabit, and govern life differently.

The work moves between writing, research, protocols, software concepts, land use, future institutions, and applied systems.
